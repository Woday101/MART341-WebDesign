Easy Peanut butter and jelly sandwhich

Ingredients

ingredients:

  • Peanut butter (crunchy or smooth, your choice, we used crunchy)
  • Jelly (flavor of your choice, we used grape)
  • Sandwich bread (white or wheat, we used white)
  • Plates to eat the sandwich on
  • Butter knife

Directions

  1. First develop a big appitite.

prep

  1. Pull Out Two Slices of Bread.
  2. Open Peanut Butter and Jelly
  3. Spread the Peanut Butter Onto One Slice of Bread
  4. Spread the Jelly Onto the Other Slice of Bread
  5. Combine the Two Slices
  6. Cut Off the Crusts (optional)
  7. Cut Into Half
  8. Clean Up Your Workspace
  9. Enjoy Your Sandwich
